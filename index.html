How to play: Use your arrow keys, WASD or HJKL to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

This is a playground for 2048, intended as an easier-to-use version of Scott Torborg's 2048 boardstate practice site. Made to work with 2048 League.

Made by YummyUranium. Base design made by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
